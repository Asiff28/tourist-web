POPULAR DESTINATIONS

HIMALAYAS

The Himalayas are the world’s highest mountain range, stretching across India, Nepal, Bhutan, China, and Pakistan. Home to Mount Everest, these majestic peaks were formed millions of years ago by tectonic collisions. The range shapes South Asia’s climate, feeds major rivers, and supports rich wildlife and vibrant cultures. A true natural wonder, the Himalayas symbolize beauty, power, and adventure.

Darjeeling

Darjeeling is a charming hill station nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas in India. Famous for its world-renowned tea and breathtaking views of Kangchenjunga, it offers a refreshing escape with cool weather, lush green hills, and colonial-era charm. Often called the “Queen of the Hills,” Darjeeling blends natural beauty, vibrant culture, and serene landscapes into an unforgettable mountain retreat.

see more
Manali

Manali is a picturesque hill station nestled in the mountains of India. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and the flowing Beas River, it is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers. From scenic landscapes and ancient temples to trekking, skiing, and river rafting, Manali offers the perfect blend of relaxation and thrill in the heart of the Himalayas.

see more
Kasol

Kasol is a serene mountain village in India, nestled along the banks of the Parvati River in Himachal Pradesh. Surrounded by lush forests and scenic Himalayan peaks, it is a favorite getaway for trekkers and backpackers. Known for its laid-back vibe, riverside cafés, and easy access to trails like Kheerganga, Kasol offers a peaceful escape into nature and adventure.
